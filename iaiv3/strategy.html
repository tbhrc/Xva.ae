Strategy

AI strategy, value impact analysis and consulting.

Strategy engagements translate interest into a plan that can withstand scrutiny. Instead of a slide deck that fades after the presentation, you receive a working roadmap and value model that can guide decisions and governance for the next twelve to twenty four months.

Readiness assessment and risk view

We begin by understanding your existing systems, data, culture and ongoing transformation efforts. This avoids designing an AI agenda that ignores the realities of your environment.

  • Interviews with key stakeholders across business and technology.
  • Review of architecture, data flows and current automated processes.
  • Identification of constraints, including security and regulatory boundaries.
  • High level maturity and readiness view for AI adoption.

Use case portfolio design

Through structured working sessions, we identify potential AI use cases across functions and score them by value, feasibility and risk. The result is a portfolio where each initiative has a clear reason to exist.

  • Detailed descriptions of candidate initiatives.
  • Identification of stakeholders, systems and data for each initiative.
  • Scoring rubric co-designed with your leadership.
  • Short list of initiatives selected for the first delivery sprints.

Value impact model and roadmap

For each chosen initiative, we build a simple, transparent value model. The objective is not to predict the future perfectly; it is to express expectations in a way that can be challenged and updated.

  • Estimates of time saved, errors reduced or additional capacity created.
  • Conversion of operational changes into conservative value ranges.
  • Portfolio view that highlights dependencies and sequencing.
  • Roadmap aligned with your budgeting and governance cycles.

Deliverables and engagement patterns

Typical strategy engagements run for four to eight weeks depending on size and complexity. They can be scoped as a one time project or as a recurring planning cycle.

  • Written strategy document and value impact summary.
  • Working roadmap with initial sprint proposals.
  • Presentation session for your leadership or board.
  • Optional transition into fractional CAIO leadership.

Input to strategy

Use readiness scores to focus the roadmap.

The readiness check highlights strengths and gaps across strategy, people, technology and governance. Those results feed directly into the strategy sprint and influence sequencing.
