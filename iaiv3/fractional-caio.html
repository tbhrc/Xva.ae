Fractional CAIO

Fractional CAIO and embedded AI departments.

Once an AI roadmap exists, the limiting factor is often capacity and coordination. A fractional Chief AI Office model gives you leadership and delivery capability without committing to a full internal department on day one.

Fractional CAIO role

The fractional CAIO acts as the accountable owner of your AI agenda, working alongside your existing executives. The role blends strategy, governance and practical guidance.

  • Participation in steering committees and key decision forums.
  • Review and refinement of proposed AI and automation initiatives.
  • Vendor and partner selection support.
  • Ongoing calibration of roadmap based on lessons learned.

Embedded delivery team

Under the CAIO, an embedded team provides the hands-on delivery capacity required for mapping, building and refining AI powered workflows.

  • Business analysts documenting processes and measuring outcomes.
  • Automation engineers building agents and integrations.
  • Quality and data specialists ensuring outputs are reliable.
  • Change support for teams adopting new ways of working.

Engagement models

Retainers are structured around time commitment and complexity. Light, standard and intensive tiers make it possible to scale engagement as your AI portfolio grows.

  • Light – suitable for organisations with a small number of active initiatives.
  • Standard – appropriate when multiple departments are engaged.
  • Intensive – for periods of heavy transformation or major programme delivery.

Transition to internal capability

Fractional arrangements are structured with a potential end state in mind. The goal is to grow your internal capacity over time.

  • Planned knowledge transfer and documentation.
  • Support in recruiting internal AI and automation roles.
  • Handover of frameworks, templates and playbooks.
  • Optional light touch advisory once your team is established.

Alignment

Use readiness scores and strategy outputs to shape the CAIO role.

The fractional CAIO engagement is anchored in your readiness profile and roadmap. This keeps the role focused on the levers that matter most.
