Workshops

AI leadership and board workshops.

Workshops create a structured environment where senior leaders can think clearly about AI without pressure to commit to specific tools or vendors. They compress months of scattered reading and conversations into one or two days of focused work.

One day executive AI briefing

A concentrated day for your C suite and senior managers covering global AI signals, UAE specific developments and practical case studies in sectors similar to yours.

  • Plain language explanations of key AI concepts and limitations.
  • Discussion of risk, controls and regulatory themes relevant to the UAE.
  • Interactive exercises where leaders identify possible use cases.
  • Documented list of questions, concerns and possible next steps.

Two day AI and operating model lab

A deeper format that first maps how work flows today, then explores where AI agents and automations can support that work. The aim is a short, prioritised list of initiatives with indicative value ranges.

  • Process mapping with operations, HR, finance and sales leaders.
  • Live demonstration of agents and automations with realistic examples.
  • Facilitated prioritisation based on impact, feasibility and risk.
  • Short written summary feeding directly into strategy design.

Who should attend

We encourage a mix of decision makers and people who understand the details of how work gets done in your organisation.

  • CEO, COO, country manager or equivalent.
  • Heads of operations, HR, finance and commercial functions.
  • CIO or senior IT leadership.
  • Selected managers already experimenting with AI or automation.

Expected outcomes

  • Shared understanding of what AI can and cannot do for your organisation in the near term.
  • Short list of credible initiatives instead of a long wish list of disconnected ideas.
  • Realistic view of the effort, governance and culture change required.
  • Clear options for moving into strategy and delivery phases.
