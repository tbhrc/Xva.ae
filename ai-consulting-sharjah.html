AI Implementation in Sharjah—Controlled & Measured

90-day delivery cadence with in-region controls, human-in-loop validation, and clear decision rights for Sharjah enterprises.

Why it works

Governed Velocity

We pair a 90-day cadence with human-in-loop controls, Sharjah data residency, and vendor allowlists. You ship AI safely and avoid shadow IT.

  • Decision rights: Steering (CIO/COO/CFO), Design Authority (CAIO + Risk), Delivery pods.
  • Human-in-loop: approve for finance, co-create for high-risk, sample for low-risk.
  • In-region: data residency in UAE; RBAC + SSO/MFA; audited prompts/models.

90-Day Cadence

From intake to operate with measurable ROI.

  • Weeks 0-2: intake top workflows, KPIs, risk tiers, guardrails.
  • Weeks 2-6: ship 3–5 workflows (finance, ops, support) with QA.
  • Weeks 6-10: harden—red-team, failover, runbooks, support training.
  • Weeks 10-12: operate—steering, ROI tracker, incident playbook.

    • Finance

    Reconciliation drafts, variance explanations, invoice QA with human approval and full audit trails.

    Operations

    Ticket summarization, exception handling, vendor comms drafts with confidence thresholds and handoff.

    Support

    Knowledge retrieval with citations, routing on low confidence, and co-pilot experiences for agents.

    Controls & Assurance

    • In-region hosting; no cross-border without approval.
    • Audit: prompts/models/versioning, output watermarking, logs.
    • Security: SSO/MFA, least-privilege RBAC, KMS-managed secrets.
    • Vendors: allowlist with failover; red-team before launch and on updates.

    Measurement

    • Efficiency: hours removed, cycle-time delta.
    • Quality: error/appeal rate, rework.
    • Financial: payback days, margin impact.
    • Adoption: human-touch %, active users, opt-outs.
